Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2026

🔐 Short version: Your API keys never leave your device. We don't sell your data. We don't track you. ExNexus is a trading tool, not a data business.

1. What We Collect

ExNexus collects minimal information to operate the service:

  • Account information — email address and name when you register
  • Usage data — anonymous feature usage to improve the platform
  • Device information — browser type and OS for compatibility

We do not collect:

  • Your API keys — stored locally on your device only
  • Your trading positions or portfolio data
  • Your financial information
  • Your location

2. Your API Keys

Exchange API keys (Bybit, Binance, Gate.io, Alpaca) are stored exclusively in your browser's local storage. They are never transmitted to ExNexus servers. All exchange API calls are made directly from your browser or through our proxy worker solely to facilitate your trades — we cannot read, copy, or store them.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve the ExNexus trading platform
  • To send product updates and important notices (with your consent)
  • To provide customer support
  • To process subscription payments

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data with:

  • Cloudflare — infrastructure and security
  • Payment processors — for subscription billing only
  • Law enforcement — only when legally required

5. Cookies & Local Storage

ExNexus uses browser local storage to save your settings, preferences, API keys (encrypted), and session tokens. No third-party tracking cookies are used. You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, secure session management, and regular security reviews. However, no system is 100% secure — we recommend using API keys with read-only or trade-only permissions (never withdrawal permissions).

7. Your Rights

  • Access — request a copy of your data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability — export your data in a standard format
  • Opt-out — unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time

8. Children's Privacy

ExNexus is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify registered users of significant changes via email. Continued use of ExNexus after changes constitutes acceptance.
